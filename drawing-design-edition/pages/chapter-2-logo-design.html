Chapter 2: Logo Design

Remake a Logo Design or Create your Own

Create a rendition of your favorite app icon or logo, or propose an original. Use design commands such as stroke, fill, strokeWeight and noStroke to add design elements to your shape commands.

Grab a piece of scratch paper or use the beginner grid below to draw out your design. Feel free to remake an existing logo or come up with your own.
